The driver accused of killing Nicki Minaj’s father in a hit-and-run last year has pleaded guilty. As you may recall, her dad Robert Maraj was walking in the village of Mineola, New York, back in February 2021 when he was hit by a 1992 Volvo Station Wagon driven by Charles Polevich. The man fled the scene after stopping to ask “if ‘he was ok.’” The 64-year-old was taken to the hospital where he succumbed to his injuries. Polevich was soon charged and later turned himself in.

Now, the 71-year-old has pleaded guilty to felony charges of leaving the scene of a fatality and tampering with physical evidence. According to Newsday, Polevich appeared in Nassau County court on Friday, where he confessed to hitting Maraj before taking off without reporting the incident to police. He also admitted to placing a tarp over his Volvo in an attempt to hide it once he returned home.
